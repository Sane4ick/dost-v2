Cargo Baskets

Dost Engineering  offers a range of offshore baskets in a several sizes suitable for a variety applications, optimized for effective and safe cargo storage and transportation. Our safe stacking design ensures the optimal utilization of often limited rig and deck floor space. All our cargo baskets have been designed with a full compliment of load securing points with additional net options to eliminate risk of lift set snagging on cargo. Custom designed and manufactured cradles can also be installed to enhance the security of fragile and high value cargo.

 All baskets are available for sale or long or short term rental or can be manufactured on shortest lead time as per your requirements.

    Key Features Designed and manufactured to DNV 2.7 – 1/ EN12079 standards Range of sizes for all standard applications Ex-stock availability for urgent requirements for short or long term hire Safe stackable design for optimized floor space Provided with internal swiveled tie down points to ensure security.

Pumps and Accessories

We offer engine or electrical explosion proof motor driven centrifugal pumps in both lower and higher pressure ranges to provide the versatility for many industrial applications. Due to their large free passage, the pumps are highly suitable for projects that require pumping of fluids such as drilling mud, sewage water or contaminated waste water. All pumps are equipped with enhanced safety features including , spark arrester, emergency stop switch and automatic level control.
